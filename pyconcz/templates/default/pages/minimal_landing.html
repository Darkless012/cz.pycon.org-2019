About PyCon CZ

PyCon CZ is the Czech Republic edition of the well-known and valued PyCon conference devoted to the Free and Open Source Python programming language.

It is organized already for the fifth time by passionate members of Czech Python community, with cooperation and underwriting by the Pyvec.org, a local non-profit organization that focuses on supporting the Python community in the Czech Republic.

To get a better idea of our previous year's events, check out our short promotional video or watch our keynotes from last year: Van Lindberg, Karen M. Sandler, Sam Trojan, Flavio Percoco and Nick Lang.

The objective of the PyCon conference is to raise participants' qualifications, and overall integration of the Python programmers' community in the Czech Republic and surrounding area, popularisation of new applications of the language as well as to spread good programming practices and exchange of experiences between professionals, novices, students and hobbyist Python programmers.

Venue

Dolní Vítkovice is a gorgeous place a national site of industrial heritage located in the centre of Ostrava. It includes an extensive industrial ironworks with former coal mine, coke ovens and blast furnace operations with exceptional technology flow and specialized machines as well as a unique collection of industrial architecture. Part of the complex is also Science and technology center or Bolt Tower. Since 2007 site is reopened and the manufacturing complex has been transformed into a unique educational, social and cultural centre of a significance reaching beyond the region.

More info at the venue’s official website.
