Workshop registration

Please read the following rules. Registrations not following them will be voided.

  1. Registration for workshops is available for PyCon CZ ticket holders only.
  2. Use the same e-mail and name you used for your ticket.
  3. You can register for Networking for Geeks (Friday 9:00)
    plus one Sunday workshop from 10:00
    plus one Sunday workshop from 14:00.
  4. There are waiting lists for full workshops.
    Please cancel your registration if you can’t come so somebody else can attend! Even on Sunday morning.
    How to cancel? There is a link to Change details page in your confirmation e-mail. Click on Cancel this ticket button and you’re done.
    • Do not join waiting lists for the same time you have registered for another workshop.
    • If you joined a waiting list, you might get an offer to register a seat as an e-mail from support@tito.io.
    • Everybody on the waiting list will be sent the offer at the same time to have a fair chance: the quickest will get a seat.
    • If anybody cancels, we will send offers even on Sunday.
