Workshop registration

Please read the following rules. Registrations not following them will be voided.

Conference attendees only

You must have a ticket to PyCon CZ to register for workshops.

Use the same e-mail and name you used for your ticket.

Registration limits

You can register for Networking for Geeks (Friday 9:00)
plus one Sunday workshop from 10:00
plus one Sunday workshop from 14:00.

Waiting lists

Please cancel your registration if you can’t come so somebody else can attend! Even on Sunday morning.

How to cancel?

There is a link to Change details page in your confirmation e-mail. Click on Cancel this ticket button and you’re done.

Joining waiting lists

Do not join waiting lists for the same time you have registered a workshop.

If you joined a waiting list, you might get an offer to register a seat as an e-mail from support@tito.io.

Everybody on the waiting list will be sent the offer at the same time to have a fair chance: the quickest will get a seat.

If the offer expires or somebody will be quicker than you don’t join the waiting list again, you're still on it.

If anybody cancels, we’ll send last offers on Sunday.
